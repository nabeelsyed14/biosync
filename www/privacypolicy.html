Privacy Policy

BioSync is a privacy-first health intelligence platform. All health data synced from Health Connect is stored locally on your device or in your own private Supabase instance.

Data Collection

We only read health metrics (Steps, Sleep, Heart Rate) to provide you with insights. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Permissions

The app requires Health Connect permissions to function. You can revoke these at any time in your device settings.
